Welcome to Shrigadi Foundation

We are an organization which is working for the overall development of School and college going students. From last 15 years in Mumbai region. In these 15 years we have been observing that our students are easily placed in the market compared to other students who does not get the benefit of Shrigadi foundation. The reason is very clear.

Our Vision

To give a life changing opportunity to students by providing life skills.

Our Mission

To bring the changes in the standard of underprivileged students, by giving them all knowledge at their right age and exploring them to the world of practical knowledge. Guiding and giving them all practical and confidence, which will take them to the right place at the right time?

Our Achievements

The activity was started to share the knowledge with the needy friends in locality in the year 2000, with 2 computers and 10 students. As the people started connecting and demanding for the exploration to other people in need Then we formed an organization called "Shrigadi foundation" and now it is reaching to 4000 students every day with 200 computers and a team of 30 well qualified and dedicated professionals for delivering the life skills program. The organization as conducted a survey door-to-door and in different Schools & Colleges in different localities and providing the selected students life skills program. From last 5 years we are organizing the annual convocation day and the certificates are given to the students on this very special day to make them realize how they are getting ready for their future.
